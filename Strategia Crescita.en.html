★ The thesis, in brief

Going multilingual doesn't just add a market: it multiplies your addressable audience from ~60M Italian speakers to over 1.5 billion speakers (EN/ES/PT/FR). But not all markets are equal: some cost 15× more to acquire a user.

The smart move: lead with Brazil and Latin America — ultra-low cost per install (~$0.34 vs $5.28 in the US), explosive growth (×5 in 5 years), and a Gen Z obsessed with astrology that loves your pop aesthetic. You grow for free with the social flywheel (the 12 mascots tagging each other), and you only pay where acquisition is cheap. English comes later, once monetisation is proven.

04 How much to invest to make your mark

Two truths to hold at once: (1) paid user acquisition is expensive, and today the average cost to acquire a customer has spiked to ~$29; (2) your app has near-zero marginal cost and viral content. So the number one lever is organic, and budget only pays where it earns its keep.

Practical rule: don't spend on paid until you know two numbers of your own — what percentage of free users convert to premium, and how long a user sticks around (30-day retention). Below a certain threshold, paid burns money; above it, it multiplies.

05 The social engine: the 12-page idea

Your instinct — one main app account plus one per sign, all tagging each other — is spot on, because the app already generates the content (horoscope per sign, daily match, "compose the phrase"). But it needs careful pacing: managing 13 accounts is a lot of work, and the algorithms penalise blanket tagging if it looks like spam.

✓ Why it works

  • Each sign account speaks to a hyper-engaged niche: people follow THEIR OWN sign.
  • The cross-tag is native to the product: @zodiac.aries posts "your match today is Libra" and tags @zodiac.libra. That's your Match feature, made social.
  • Infinite, automatic content from the app: 12 horoscopes/day + match cards + phrases.

⚠ Watch out

  • 13 accounts = a lot of work, and new accounts start with very low reach.
  • The same tags every day across all accounts = risk of a spam flag.
  • Risk of losing focus if the "master" account doesn't have a strong, consistent voice.

The recommended plan (phased)

  • Phase 1 — 1 master + 4 elements. One strong account @zodiac.zine + 4 accounts for the elements (🔥Fire, 🌍Earth, 💨Air, 💧Water). Less overhead than 12, but already segmented. The master reposts the best content; the element accounts tag each other for compatibility posts.
  • Phase 2 — the 12 signs. Once the master has traction and the content machine is running smoothly, open the 12 sign accounts. Cross-tag only where it makes sense (the sign + its daily match), never as a blanket.
  • Formats that perform: "your match today" (shareable card), "compose the phrase" (meme), the mascots as stickers/GIFs, and the daily horoscope in 9:16. All already produced by the app.
  • The exact same approach on TikTok (where organic reach for new accounts is currently the highest): 9:16 horoscope/match videos featuring the animated mascots.
In one line: yes to the idea, but start with 5 accounts (1+4), not 13. The value isn't the number of pages — it's that every post is a free mini-ad for the app, and the Match feature turns cross-tagging into natural content rather than spam.

06 Do Instagram and TikTok pay you?

Yes, once you hit certain thresholds — but creator payments are small. The real value of social isn't that money: it's the free installs and subscribers you drive to the app.

Uncomfortable truth: with these numbers, creator payments barely cover the work. Example: a viral Reel with 1M views earns you ~$400–1,000 from the TikTok programme… but if that Reel drives 5,000 installs, it's worth far more (at $0.34–5 each in saved acquisition cost, plus the subscribers). Treat creator monetisation as a bonus, not the business. The business is the app.

Creator monetisation availability: active in Italy, France, Spain, Brazil, USA/UK and many more — so it covers all your markets.

★ In summary

Going multilingual doesn't double the potential: it multiplies it and makes it cheaper to achieve, by opening Brazil and LatAm — where astrology is exploding and acquiring a user costs a fraction. Strategy: win for free through social (1 master + 4 elements, then the 12 signs), pay only where it's cheap, and treat creator payments as a bonus. English is the final trophy, not the starting point.
